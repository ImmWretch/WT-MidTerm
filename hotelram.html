Hotel Tamilnadu Rameshwaram

An 8-minute walk from Ramanathaswamy Temple, this casual hotel opposite the sea is also a 5-minute walk from Agni Tirtham (a holy bathing area) and a 10-minute walk from Sangumal Beach. The unassuming rooms come with low-key wood furnishings; some face the sea, while others have air-conditioning. There are also themed rooms and suites, and upgraded units add additional living space. Suites also feature sitting areas, including sofas. Breakfast is complimentary. There's an informal restaurant with whitewashed walls and sitting areas, as well as a bar.
