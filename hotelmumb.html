Hotels to stay in Mumbai

The Taj Mahal Palace

Opposite the Arabian Sea, this luxury hotel is a 6-minute walk from the Gateway of India and 3 km from the bustling Marine Drive. The refined rooms come with free Wi-Fi, flat-screens and turndown service. Club-level rooms add butler service, marble bathrooms and access to a private lounge serving drinks and appetizers. Suites add living rooms, and the presidential suite provides a spa and a fitness room. Freebies include a breakfast buffet, parking and airport pickup (for club-level and suitesâ€™ guests). There are 11 upscale restaurants and bars. A spa, a beauty salon, an outdoor pool, a fitness room and a luxe shopping zone are also on-site.

St.Regis Hotel

Adjoining the upscale Palladium shopping centre, this lavish, high-rise hotel is a 14-minute walk from the Lower Parel railway and monorail station, and 3 km from Shree Siddhivinayak Temple. Elegant rooms come with free Wi-Fi and flat-screen TVs, plus minibars, iPod docks and desks. Suites add living rooms; upgraded suites include kitchenettes and/or office spaces. Butler service is available. Parking is complimentary. There are 5 sleek restaurants, including 1 on the 40th floor, plus a regal bar, a cocktail lounge and a nightclub. Other amenities include a gym, and an outdoor pool with cabanas, as well as a spa, a salon and meeting/event spaces.

Four Seasons Hotel

Set 3 km from the Siddhivinayak Temple, this plush high-rise hotel with a glass facade is 8 km from both Girgaum Chowpatty beach and Mahatma Jyotiba Phule Mandai market. Contemporary rooms with floor-to-ceiling windows feature flat-screen TVs, free Wi-Fi and safes, plus minibars, and tea and coffeemaking facilities. Upgraded rooms add sea views. Suites include living rooms. Room service is available. Free amenities include breakfast and parking. There's an upscale Pan-Asian restaurant, a Mediterranean eatery, and a rooftop pool with a terrace. Other amenities include a gym and a spa, plus a business centre and event space. Pets are welcome.

Fairmont

Fairmont Mumbai is a luxury hotel in Andheri East, Mumbai, known for its blend of Art Deco design and modern amenities. It offers 446 spacious guest rooms and suites, a variety of dining options, and a range of amenities including a fitness center, spa, and event spaces. The hotel is also known for its commitment to guest satisfaction and its unique longevity suite, Blu Xone, offering advanced age-defying therapies.
